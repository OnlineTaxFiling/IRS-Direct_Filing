FAQ – Filing Taxes Worldwide

Q: Can I file taxes online for free in 2026?
A: Yes, in many countries (like the US via IRS Free File partners) if your income and situation are simple. Always check your local tax authority for free e‑file rules.

Q: How fast will I get my tax refund?
A: Most countries offer 2–3 week refunds with direct deposit if you e‑file early and avoid errors.

Q: What documents do I need to file taxes?
A: Typically: ID, income documents (W‑2, 1099, payslips), receipts for deductions, prior‑year tax info, and a local e‑file account.
