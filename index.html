Yes. Many permanent USA residents and visa holders with simple 1040 returns under $100k income qualify for IRS Free File via E‑file.com. This works even if you’re currently living abroad.

❓ How fast are IRS refunds in 2026?

With direct deposit, the IRS aims to issue refunds in about 21 days. E‑file + direct deposit is typically around 50% faster than paper filing, whether you’re in the US or abroad.

❓ Need a tax extension in 2026 because you’re abroad?

If you are a US citizen, green card holder, permanent resident, or visa holder living outside the US, you may automatically get a 2‑month extension past April 15. For extra peace of mind, some use an extension bundle via TaxExtension.com to extend to October 15, 2026.

❓ Do I still need to file US taxes if I live abroad?

Yes, in many cases. US citizens, green card holders, and certain permanent residents must file US taxes on worldwide income. Visa holders may also need to file if they have US‑source income (e.g., wages, freelance work for US companies). Use IRS‑authorized e‑file to file from anywhere.
